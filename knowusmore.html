Meet Our Director

The Director of S'bali Driving Academy, Mr Eric Samkethe Nkosi

"S'bali" as his close associates call him is a committed and passionate person who has significantly aided in the growth of skilled drivers in South Africa. His driving school, S'bali Driving Academy, was founded in 2020 after National Lockdown with the intention of offering reasonably priced driving instruction and individualized training to meet the needs of each student.

Eric's dedication to a driver's overall development, with a focus on defensive driving techniques to save lives and avoid accidents, served as the impetus for opening the driving academy. He makes sure that the learners feel encouraged and appreciated through the learning process by emphasizing the significance of compassion and understanding in his teaching style.

In addition to teaching driving, S'bali Driving Academy has supported impoverished areas by generating jobs. Due to the academy's dedication to quality control and client satisfaction, it has developed a solid reputation and drawn customers from all around Pretoria. People from all walks of life can now more easily access the academy's services thanks to its accessibility by public transportation and pick-up and drop-off services.

Eric has been involved in a number of projects focused at increasing road safety and lowering accident rates in addition to his position at Sbali Driving Academy. In addition to making his students' lives better, his initiatives have increased road safety in South Africa as a whole..
